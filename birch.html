The Birch Tree

They’re perfect trees to plant in our New York and Connecticut climate — the white birch tree is actually native to the Connecticut River Valley. These trees need plenty of sunlight and thrive in well-draining, moist soil heavy on the sand, silt, and clay. Under the right conditions, the trees can grow up to 70 feet tall, with a 35-foot spread. They’re beautiful additions to New York and Connecticut landscapes!
